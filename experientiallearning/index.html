BMCC Spotlight Series

July 20 at 3:00 pm - 4:00 pm | Zoom
Join BMCC’s Spotlight Series sessions to talk directly to an Admissions Representative about college questions and academic options. You’ll also discover the different support services BMCC offers to all students.BMCC provides students with services to become successful both inside and outside of the classroom. IHOPE Program Information Session

July 20 at 5:30 pm - 6:30 pm | Zoom
Want to start your pathway to a career in the fast-growing field of healthcare or information technology? In a matter of months, you can earn a certificate and create new possibilities for your family and your future through the IHOPE program. We are looking for single head-of-household parents or guardians living in Upper Manhattan or the Bronx. If you have any questions, email us at naristy@bmcc.cuny.edu or call (212) 776-6616.

July 19 at 2:30 pm - 3:45 pm

Year Up is a one-year intensive program for students pursuing an associate degree that combines hands-on skill development and internships at some of America’s top companies.
