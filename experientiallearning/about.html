ABOUT OUR WEBSITE

Welcome to our comprehensive online platform designed to support BMCC students in their career readiness journey. With a genuine passion for helping students succeed at BMCC, we have developed a solution that addresses the common challenges faced by students regarding career readiness, such as opportunities going unnoticed due to a lack of knowledge about resources around them.

Our mission is to enhance students' access to opportunities, provide personalized support, and foster their career success. By harnessing the power of data analytics and machine learning techniques, we have created a personalized resource that will guide students throughout their undergraduate career. We understand that each student is unique, and through our platform, we offer a variety of resources to help them navigate career options effectively. Whether it's finding the right job or internship, preparing for interviews, or exploring BMCC community resources, our platform provides convenient accessibility to comprehensive information in a structured manner.

In the future, our primary focus is on enhancing our chatbot, making it a true personal mentor for each student. We envision a chatbot capable of providing personalized recommendations and tailored suggestions based on individual skills and aspirations. But that's not all; we aim to enrich the student experience further. We're dedicated to expanding the resources available on our website, empowering students on their career readiness journey

Meet Our Mentors

We would like to express our sincere gratitude to Professor Azhar and Professor Younes for their guidance and support throughout the development of this website. Special acknowledgment to Sam and Benjamin

Dr. Mohammad Azhar

Dr. Azhar is an Associate Professor of Computer Information Systems at CUNY BMCC. His current research includes human-robot collaboration for assistive robotics, cybersecurity education, and educational robotics. Dr. Azhar has been involved in organizing and teaching numerous teacher training workshops for Educational Robotics programs, after-school robotics clubs, and robotics competitions for students over a decade.

Prof. Younes Benkarroum

Professor Benkarroum is an Assistant Professor of Computer Information Systems at CUNY BMCC. His research interests lie in the fields of computer vision and 3D reconstruction. His main research incorporates a variety of aspects, including mathematics, computer science, and biology.
