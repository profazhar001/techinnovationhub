New student Community

Description :
Our solution for the new student community is a comprehensive website that provides all the necessary information and resources related to their degree work in their majors.

Here are the key features and functionalities of our solution:

Curriculum search function:

One of the key features of our website is a robust curriculum search function. This allows students to quickly search for specific courses or requirements within their major. They can easily find information about course availability, prerequisites, course descriptions, and schedules. This feature aims to save students time and reduce the chances of missing important registration deadlines by providing a user-friendly and efficient way to navigate the curriculum.

Quick assistance:

We prioritize providing quick and efficient assistance to students through our website. This includes features such as a live chat support system, where students can connect with advisors or knowledgeable staff members to get immediate help with their queries or concerns. We also offer a comprehensive FAQ section that addresses common questions and issues students may encounter, allowing them to find answers without the need for direct assistance.

Data Analysis Visualization:

To aid students in selecting their majors within the CIS department at BMCC, our solution includes a dedicated page featuring a set of data analysis visualizations. These visualizations provide an overview of the majors offered, including information on enrollment trends, employment prospects, average salaries, and other relevant data points. By presenting this information in an easily understandable format, students can get a quick glance and gain insights into different majors, helping them make more informed decisions about their academic path.

Majors and Curriculum Search

Course choices options and suggestions: this allows students to explore and select their desired courses. It provides a comprehensive list of available courses, along with relevant information such as course descriptions, prerequisites, and schedules. Additionally, our application offers personalized suggestions based on students' academic goals, interests, and previous course selections, helping them make informed decisions.
